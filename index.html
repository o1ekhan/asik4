About

Who are we?

We are a student club in AITU, that aspires young musicians to express themselves,
and makes the student life in the university all the more interesting,
by making colorful and exciting concerts within its walls!

What do we do?

The main focus of our Music Club is making great live shows in our university.
We play songs of many different genres, from heavy rock to soothing pop and r&b

Join us!

If you are a musician in Astana IT University,
we would be glad for you to join our club!
